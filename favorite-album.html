My Favorite Album: The New Abnormal by The Strokes

"The New Abnormal" is The Strokes' acclaimed sixth album, released in 2020 after a seven-year hiatus. It combines their signature indie rock and post-punk styles with catchy hooks and introspective lyrics, marking a triumphant return for the band. Notable tracks include "Bad Decisions" and "At the Door."
